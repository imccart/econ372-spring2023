Module 3, Part 4: Hospital Mergers

We made it! This is our last week of new content. We’ll finish this semester with a nice discussion of mergers and prices, interpreting this literature in the context of the negotiation model we’ve covered previously.

Objectives for this week

  • Summarize the existing data on hospital mergers and their effects on hospital prices and quality
  • Differentiate between in- and out-of-market mergers
