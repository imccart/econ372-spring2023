This week we continue our coverage of hospital pricing, focusing on the role of competition and mergers.

Objectives for this week

  • Explain the role of competition on pricing and describe how we measure competition in health care

Activities

  • Review data from the Hospital Cost Report Information System (HCRIS)
  • Discuss Zack Cooper et al. “The Price Ain’t Right? Hospital Prices and Health Spending on the Privately Insured,” The Quarterly Journal of Economics 134, no. 1 (2019): 51–107.
